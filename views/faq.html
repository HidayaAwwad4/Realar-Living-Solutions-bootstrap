Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services and platform.

How do I list my property on Realar?

Simply sign up as an agency or owner and fill out the listing form with details and photos. Our team will review and publish it promptly.

Do you offer virtual tours?

Yes, many of our listings include 3D virtual tours. You can filter properties that support this feature while browsing.

Is there a fee to join as an agency?

No, registration is free. However, we offer premium subscription options with more visibility and features.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us anytime via the Contact Us page. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
